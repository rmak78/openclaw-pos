# Global POS+ERP Glossary

## A
- **Adjustment**: A non-sale modification applied to inventory or financial balances with audit reason.

## B
- **Branch**: A physical retail location operating one or more tills.
- **Batch Close (Payments)**: Closing card terminal batch for settlement processing.

## C
- **Cash Variance**: Difference between expected and counted cash at shift close.
- **Cashier**: Till operator handling transactions.
- **Conflict (Sync)**: Mismatch discovered when offline transactions are replayed to central services.

## E
- **End-of-Shift Report**: Reconciliation output summarizing sales, tenders, refunds, and variance.

## F
- **Float (Opening Float)**: Starting cash amount assigned to till at shift start.

## I
- **Idempotency Key**: Unique key used to ensure retried transaction submissions are not duplicated.
- **Incident Log**: Structured record of operational or technical issues in a branch.

## O
- **Offline Mode**: Controlled mode allowing limited operations without central connectivity.

## R
- **Reconciliation**: Process of matching operational transactions with financial/system records.
- **Recovery Queue**: Set of failed sync items awaiting retry or manual action.

## S
- **Shift**: Time-bound operating period assigned to a cashier/till.
- **Shift Supervisor**: Role authorizing sensitive actions, variances, and closures.
- **Sync Recovery**: Process to replay offline transactions and resolve conflicts after reconnect.

## T
- **Till**: POS terminal/device used for transaction processing.
- **Till Health Check**: Pre-shift validation of key components (printer, scanner, payment link, sync state).

## U
- **Uptime Window**: Period where branch/till services are expected to be available.

## V
- **Variance Threshold**: Configured value beyond which supervisor/manager approval is mandatory.
